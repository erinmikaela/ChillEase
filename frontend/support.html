Frequently Asked Questions

How do I link my PhilHealth ID?

Open your account settings in QueueEase and enter your PhilHealth number there.

What happens after I join a PhilHealth queue?

You receive a confirmation message and your queue number appears in the app.

Can I update my PhilHealth details later?

Yes just go to your profile in QueueEase and update your PhilHealth information.

How do I cancel my PhilHealth queue spot?

Open the queue section and tap cancel to remove your number from the list.

Does QueueEase work with all PhilHealth branches?

QueueEase supports every branch that uses the standard PhilHealth queue API.

Will I get a notification when it is my turn?

Yes QueueEase will notify you in the app when PhilHealth is ready to serve you.

Can I join another service while waiting?

Yes you can switch services and rejoin any additional PhilHealth queue.

How is my wait time estimated?

QueueEase uses live data from PhilHealth to calculate your estimated wait time.

Is there any cost for PhilHealth queueing?

QueueEase is completely free for managing your PhilHealth queues.

Who can I contact for queue support?

Tap the live chat icon to connect with our support team at any time.

What information do I need to provide?

You only need your PhilHealth number and your full name to join the queue.

Can I access my queue history?

Yes open the history tab in QueueEase to review your past queue records at PhilHealth.
